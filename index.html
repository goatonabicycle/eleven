Eleven

A solitaire card game played with a standard 52-card deck on a 3×3 grid.

How to Play

  • Deal cards one at a time onto the 3×3 grid
  • As soon as a valid match appears, replace those cards with the next cards from the deck
  • Continue dealing until all 9 positions are filled
  • Keep matching and replacing until no moves remain

Valid Matches

  • Pairs that sum to 11: A+10, 2+9, 3+8, 4+7, 5+6
  • Face card trio: J+Q+K (any suits)

Win / Lose

  • Win: The draw pile is emptied
  • Lose: No valid matches while cards remain in the pile
